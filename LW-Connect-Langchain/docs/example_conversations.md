## Conversation 3: Quick Query

**User**: What's the difference between a cohort and a learning pathway?

**Assistant**: Here's the distinction:

**Cohort**: A group of learners who progress through a program together, with scheduled sessions, peer interaction, and often a specific start/end date. Think of it as a "class" with classmates.

**Learning Pathway**: A structured sequence of courses or modules you can take at your own pace. It's self-directed and flexible, focusing on skill progression rather than group learning.

Many programs combine both - you might follow a learning pathway within a cohort structure to get both flexibility and community support.

## Key Conversation Patterns

### 1. Clarification
- Ask follow-up questions when user intent is unclear
- Offer multiple options to narrow down needs

### 2. Contextualization  
- Reference previous messages in the conversation
- Build on established context

### 3. Actionability
- Always provide clear next steps
- Offer specific recommendations, not just information

### 4. Transparency
- Acknowledge limitations when information isn't available
- Explain reasoning behind recommendations

### 5. Conciseness
- Keep responses under 150 words unless detail is requested
- Use bullet points for clarity
- Prioritize most relevant information
